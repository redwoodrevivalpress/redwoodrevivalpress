Increasing access to the treasures of the past.

We publish recovered and re-examined work from earlier eras — autobiography, letters, classical rhetoric, regional history — and original scholarship that takes the long view of subjects most readers know only from their recent surface. Our aim is simple: bring the durable parts of the past forward in editions readers will actually open.
